About Us

About Us

E-Zing Overseas Trade & Travel is a dynamic and versatile company offering a broad spectrum of services. Initially, in year 2009, established as a specialist in visa and immigration-related services, E-Zing has grown to include a thriving import-export business later on in year 2013. Our dual focus on facilitating global mobility and trading essential goods demonstrates our commitment to excellence and adaptability in diverse markets. Since 2018, E-Zing has entered into collaboration with Global Movers Consultants (GMC), a firm that offers a broad spectrum of services designed to cater to diverse immigration needs.

At E-Zing Overseas Trade & Travel, our mission is to provide seamless, efficient, and comprehensive solutions across both immigration services and international trade. We are dedicated to facilitating global opportunities through expert visa and immigration support.

E-Zing is actively involved in providing core immigration services like visa assistance, immigration consultation, application support, document services, post-visa support etc.

E-Zing Overseas is delivering high-quality fastener items and spices to meet the diverse needs of our clients worldwide. We are involved in import and export of a wide variety of fastener items (including bolts, nuts, screws, washers) and premium spices (black pepper, cumin, turmeric, and cardamom).

We aim to drive success and build lasting partnerships by upholding integrity, innovation, and exceptional service. Our 6 members team of Educational consultancy and 5 members team of foreign trade are very liable, experienced and highly efficient in their work.
